Product Shoot

Photography/Layout

For this project, I created a perfume photoshoot with a high-fashion magazine spread featuring the fragrance. I took all of the photos myself, carefully selecting the lighting, props, and backgrounds to capture the essence of the fragrance and convey its mood and feeling.

The photos are the centerpiece of the magazine spread, with large, high-quality images of the perfume bottle and accompanying elements like flowers or other props. The layout is minimalistic, allowing the photos to take center stage and showcase the beauty and appeal of the fragrance.
