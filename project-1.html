Gray’s School of Art / Graphic Design / Layout

A Reimagined Charter Journey

Communication Design Tutor Annette challenged us to redesign the Gray's School of Art Student & Staff Charter, with one design ultimately being chosen for the final version. Three students including myself took on the challenge. Recognising the existing design's lack of visual appeal for art students, we focused on incorporating updated copy into layouts that were both informative and visually engaging. My approach involved crafting each spread with a unique design, ensuring users remained stimulated and actively interacted with the content, thus celebrating the school's vibrant creative spirit.

All Photography used in the spreads of this project are provided by The Gatehouse.

01

Crafting the ideal School of Art charter cover wasn't a straight line. I delved into both classic styles aligning with the university's image and bolder, unconventional options designed to pique interest and spark curiosity. This exploration broadened the range of possible outcomes, ultimately leading to the darker, more unconventional cover. This cover embodies the School of Art's spirit of pushing boundaries and inspiring creativity through unconventional means.
